# 🔵 Carrier Signal

👉   **একটা high-frequency pure signal (usually sine wave), যেটা information বহন করে**

---

## 🎯 Example

- Radio carrier: 1 MHz, 100 MHz

---

## 🧠 Characteristics

- high frequency
- no information initially
- easy to transmit (antenna friendly)

---

# 🔥 Core Relation

👉  
**Message signal carrier এর উপর “ride” করে**

---

## 🎯 Visualization
