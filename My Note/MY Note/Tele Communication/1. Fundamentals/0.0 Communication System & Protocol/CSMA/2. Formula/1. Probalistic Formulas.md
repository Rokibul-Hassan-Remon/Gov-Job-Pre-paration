
```text
N = total number of stations
p = probability that ONE station transmits
```

প্রত্যেক station independently decide করে।

---

# STEP 1 — Probability of Transmit

একটা station transmit করার probability:

$$P(Transmit)=p$$

---

# STEP 2 — Probability of NOT Transmit

একটা station transmit না করার probability:

$$P(Not\ Transmit) = 1-p $$

---

# STEP 3 — Probability of Idle

# Idle মানে:

কেউ transmit করছে না।

অর্থাৎ:  
সব N stations wait করছে।

---

# Since Independent

প্রত্যেক station এর probability:

$$(1-p)$$



আরও compact style:

$$\begin{aligned}  
P(\text{Idle})  
&= \underbrace{(1-p)(1-p)(1-p)\cdots(1-p)}_{N\text{ times}} \\  
&= (1-p)^N  
\end{aligned}  $$

---

# Mental Model

```text
	A waits
	B waits
	C waits
	D waits
```

সবাই wait করলে channel idle।

---

# Example

If:

```text
	N = 3
	p = 0.2
```

Then:
$$
\begin{aligned}
P(Idle) 
& = (0.8)^3 \\
& =0.512
\end {aligned}
$$

Meaning:  
`51.2%` chance nobody transmits।

---

# STEP 4 — Probability of Successful Transmission

# Success মানে:

exactly ONE station transmits।

---

# One Particular Station

ধরো A transmit করবে: `p`

বাকি `(N-1)` stations wait করবে:

$$(1-p)^{N-1}$$

Combined:

$$p(1-p)^{N-1}$$

---

# But Any One Can Succeed

Total N possible successful stations আছে।

So:

$$P(Success)=Np(1-p)^{N-1}$$

---

# Mental Model

```text
	Only ONE talks
	everyone else silent
```

---

# Example

If:

```text
	N = 4
	p = 0.2
```


Then:

$$
P(Success)=4(0.2)(0.8)^3 \\
=0.4096
$$

≈ 41%

---

# STEP 5 — Probability of Collision

# Collision মানে:

two or more stations transmit simultaneously।

---

# Total Probability Always

$$P(All \:\:\: possible) = P(Total) = 1$$

এখন:

$$Total = Idle + Success + Collision$$

So:

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \qquad P(Total)=P(Collision) + P(Idle) + P(Success)\\
\\
& => P(Collision) = P(Total) - P(Idle) - P(Success)\\ \\
& => P(Collision)=1-(1-p)^N-Np(1-p)^{N-1}
\end{aligned}
$$

---

# Final Formula

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \qquad P(Collision)=p^N \\ \\
& => P(Collision)=1-(1-p)^N-Np(1-p)^{N-1}
\end{aligned}
$$

---

# Example

Using:

```text
	N = 4
	p = 0.2
```

We already found:

```text
	P(Idle)=0.4096
	P(Success)=0.4096
```

So:

$$P(Collision)=1-0.4096-0.4096$$

$$=0.1808$$

≈ 18%

---

# STEP 6 — Probability of NOT Collision

Now very important।

# “Not collision” means:

Either:

- Idle  
    OR
    
- Successful transmission
    

---

So:

$$P(No\ Collision)=P(Idle)+P(Success)$$

---

# Final Formula

$$P(No\ Collision)=(1-p)^N+Np(1-p)^{N-1}$$

---

# OR Simply

$$P(No\ Collision)=1-P(Collision)$$

---

# FINAL SUMMARY TABLE 🔥

| Event                                  | Formula                                      |
| -------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Probability of transmit                | $$p$$                                        |
| Probability of not transmit            | $$1-p$$                                      |
| Probability of idle                    | $$(1-p)^N$$                                  |
| Probability of successful transmission | $$Np(1-p)^{N-1}$$                            |
| Probability of collision               | $$1-(1-p)^N-Np(1-p)^{N-1}$$<br>Or<br>$$P^N$$ |
| Probability of no collision            | $$(1-p)^N+Np(1-p)^{N-1}$$                    |
