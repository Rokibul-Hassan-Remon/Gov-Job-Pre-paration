
# 🔴 Nyquist Rule (base concept)

👉  Sampling rate$$ f_s ≥ 2 f_{max}$$

👉 এখানে:

- $$f_{max} $$= signal এর highest frequency

---

# 🟢 1) Perfect Sampling (Nyquist Sampling)

👉 Condition:

$$f_s = 2 f_{max}$$

👉 Meaning:

- ঠিক যতটা দরকার, ঠিক ততটাই sample নেওয়া

✔️ Result:

- signal perfectly reconstruct করা যায়
- no aliasing

---

# 🔵 2) Over Sampling

👉 Condition:

$$f_s > 2 f_{max}​$$

👉 Meaning:

- প্রয়োজনের চেয়ে বেশি sample নেওয়া

✔️ Result:

- reconstruction খুব accurate
- noise handling ভালো
- aliasing risk নাই

❌ Downside:

- data size বেশি
- bandwidth waste

---

# 🔴 3) Under Sampling

👉 Condition:

$$f_s < 2 f_{max}$$

👉 Meaning:

- প্রয়োজনের চেয়ে কম sample নেওয়া

❌ Result:

- aliasing হয়
- signal distortion
- original signal recover করা যায় না

---

# 📊 Comparison Table

| Type    | Condition           | Result               |
| ------- | ------------------- | -------------------- |
| Perfect | $$f_s = 2f_{max}$$  | ideal reconstruction |
| Over    | $$f_s > 2f_{max}$$  | safe, high quality   |
| Under   | $$f_s < 2f_{max}$$​ | aliasing, distortion |

---

# 🧠 Visual intuition

ধরো তুমি video তুলছো:

- 🎥 **Under sampling** → frame কম → video jumpy
- 🎥 **Perfect sampling** → smooth
- 🎥 **Over sampling** → ultra smooth (but heavy file)
