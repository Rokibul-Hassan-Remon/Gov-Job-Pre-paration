# 1. AM signal

$$s(t)=A_c(1+m\cos\omega_m t)\cos\omega_c t$$

এখানে:

 - $$ (A_c) = carrier amplitude$$
    
- $$(m) = modulation index$$
    
- $$(\omega_c) = carrier frequency$$
    
- $$(\omega_m) = message frequency$$
---

# 2. Power concept (basic idea)

Power of a sinusoid:

$$P=\frac{A^2}{2R}$$

👉 তাই AM signal এর power বের করতে আমরা amplitude অংশ square করি এবং average নেই।

---

# 3. AM Power breakdown

AM signal এর 3টা component থাকে:

### (A) Carrier power
$$P_c=\frac{A_c^2}{2R}$$

---

### (B) Sideband power (message carrying part)

Each sideband power:

$$P_{USB}=P_{LSB}=\frac{m^2 A_c^2}{8R}$$
Total power of sideband :

$$P_{USB} + P_{LSB}=\frac{m^2 A_c^2}{8R} + \frac{m^2 A_c^2}{8R} = \frac{m^2 A_c^2}{4R}$$

---

# 4. Total AM power
$$P_t=P_c\left(1+\frac{m^2}{2}\right)$$

---

# 5. Meaning of result

## Total power =
	
- Carrier power (main part)
    
-  Sideband power (information part)
    

---

# 6. Important observation

### Carrier power:

- carries NO information
    
- still consumes most power
    

### Sidebands:

- carry actual message
    
- but less power
    

---

# 7. Efficiency (very important)

$$\eta=\frac{P_{sideband}}{P_t}=\frac{m^2}{2+m^2}$$

---

# 8. Special cases

### (A) No modulation (m = 0)
	
- Only carrier exists x(c) = `Acos(wt)`
    
- No information x(m) = 0
    

---

### (B) 100% modulation (m = 1)

$$\eta_{max}=\frac{1}{3}\approx 33 \%$$
👉 Only 33% useful power

---

### (C) Over modulation (m > 1)

- distortion occurs
    
- envelope breaks
    

---

# 9. Key intuition

👉 AM power waste is because:

> carrier consumes power but carries no information

---

# 10. One-line summary

`AM total power = carrier power + sideband power,` 
	where only sidebands carry information and efficiency is low (~33% max).
