# DC Component Problem and Capacitor Relation

## First Physical Intuition

### Capacitor with DC Source ( Basic Circuit Understanding )

আমরা জানি, যদি একটি capacitor-এর সাথে DC voltage source connect করা হয়, তাহলে শুরুতে কিছু সময় current flow হয় কারণ capacitor charge হতে থাকে।

কিন্তু কিছু সময় পরে:

```text
	capacitor voltage = source voltage
```

হয়ে যায়।

---

## Then What Happens?

-  Current Stops `( ckt , I = 0)`

#### Final State

তখন আর current flow হয় না।

কারণ capacitor fully charged হয়ে যায়।

---

## Physical Meaning

### Capacitor Behavior

#### Important Intuition

Capacitor steady DC current পছন্দ করে না।

এটি শুধু:

```text
  changing signal
```

বা

```text
  alternating signal
```

ভালো  pass করতে  পারে।

---

# NOW CONNECT THIS TO COMMUNICATION SYSTEM

## Unipolar NRZ Example

### Long Sequence of 1

#### 

```text
Input Data: 111111111111

Electrical Signal: +V ─────────────────────────── (Signal is constant)
```

# Therefore Physically
	
-  Signal Behaves Like DC
	

### Why Behave like DC?

#### Intuition

কারণ changing waveform নেই।

তাই signal DC voltage-এর মতো behave করতে শুরু করে।

---

# Now What Happens in Receiver/Channel?

## Capacitor Trouble Starts

### Physical Effect

#### Important Reason

Communication channel বা receiver-এর capacitor এই constant voltage pass করতে পারে না।

কারণ capacitor কিছু সময় পরে fully charged হয়ে যায়।

তখন:

```text
no current flows
```

---

# Therefore

## Receiver Gets Distorted Signal

### Final Problem

#### Result

Receiver incoming signal-এর actual voltage properly বুঝতে পারে না।

ফলে:

- waveform distortion
    
- detection error
    
- baseline wandering
    

হতে পারে।

---

# NOW THE MATHEMATICAL VIEW

## DC Frequency

### Mathematical Definition

DC component-এর frequency: `f=0`

---

# Capacitor Impedance Formula

$$X_c=\frac1{2\pi fC}$$

---

# Now Put DC Frequency

## Calculation

If: `f=0`

then:
$$X_c=\frac1{2\pi(0)C}$$

---

# Final Result

## Infinite Impedance

### Mathematical Meaning

$$X_c\to\infty$$

---

# Physical Meaning of Infinite Impedance

 - No Current Flow
	
 - Capacitor Blocking
	
## Important Interpretation

Infinite impedance মানে:

```text
  capacitor behaves like open circuit
```

তাই:

```text
  DC current cannot pass
```

---

# Therefore Final Core Understanding

## Why DC Component Is a Problem?

-  Physical Reason
	
-  Complete Logic
	

**Long constant voltage region creates DC component.**

DC component means:` f=0`

#### Case : `f=0`

capacitor impedance becomes:

$$X_c\to\infty$$

Therefore:

```text
capacitor blocks the signal
```

which creates:

- signal distortion
    
- receiver interpretation problem
    
- baseline wandering
    
- data detection error
